Greetings!

Lakbyte Games is dedicated to creating memorable and immersive video game experiences that captivate players and stand out in the ever-evolving gaming industry. With a focus on delivering engaging and innovative content, our company is committed to pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment.

Why Lakbyte?

The name Lakbyte Games symbolizes our progress in game development, with the term "byte" representing each step we take forward. It also reflects the Filipino word "lakbay," meaning journey. Meanwhile, the word "games" highlights our focus on creating fun and enjoyable gaming experiences for players of all ages.

Who are we?

We are developers based in the Philippines, driven by creativity and a commitment to excellence. Our passionate and dynamic team is dedicated to pushing boundaries and exploring new ideas. We focus on delivering exceptional gaming experiences that captivate and make a lasting impact in the gaming world.

PROJECTS

ITS RESTLESS: FRAGMENTS
 In this first installment, unravel the shattered reality of a student cursed to live his nightmares. As fragmented visions of his past and fears come to life, he must piece together clues and confront his deepest anxieties to survive the initial horrors of the curse.

ITS RESTLESS: DESCENDING
 The journey deepens as the student descends further into the abyss of his nightmares. The boundaries between reality and nightmare blur, presenting increasingly sinister challenges. As he ventures into the darkest corners of his mind, he must confront the most terrifying aspects of his psyche to find a way out.

ITS RESTLESS: RECKONING
 In the final chapter, the student faces the ultimate reckoning. The culmination of his trials brings him to a climactic confrontation with the source of his curse. To escape his torment and reclaim his sanity, he must confront the true nature of his nightmares and make a harrowing choice.

At Lakbyte Games, our team is led by the Developer Team, which handles coding, debugging, and maintenance. Anchored by the pivotal Designer Team, which creates all visual assets and marketing materials to ensure a cohesive and captivating aesthetic, and supported by the Music Composers, who enhance the experience with immersive sound effects and music.
